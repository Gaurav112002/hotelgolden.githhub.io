Our Hotel

Golden Hotel is located in the beautiful city of Jaipur just 750 m from Sindhi Camp Bus Station, 2.2 km from Nahargarh Fort, 2 km from Jaipur Railway Station, 12.5 km from Jaipur International Airport. Guest can visit near by excursion like Nahargarh Fort (2.2 km), City Palace (3.3 km), Hawa Mahal (3.3 km), Jantar Mantar (3.4 km) Birla Mandir (4.9 km) and many more.

Golden Hotel offers budget facilities and was started as far back as 1979. The hotel is strategically placed next to Victory Cinema and on Station Road amidst a fast paced market area.

The ornate balconies and bay windows, tastefully done rooms, bricked poolside and the hand carved reception desk surrounded by stone pillars add to the royal holiday experience.
